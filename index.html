Animals

Animals are found in every country on earth. Their unique characteristics can be captured in a blink of an eye or in one shot of the camera but can be remembered forever. We can learn from them, imitate them and most of all, protect them. Browse our collection taken from the Amazon Rainforest, South America, Thailand, Tenerife and England.

People

Perhaps the most intriguing creature to planet earth. Our emotions are revealed in our eyes, mouth, face muscles, body language and speech. A photo can capture all of those beautiful facets that make us human. A different Culture, Language, Country, Race, Tribe or Family does not change our humanity. Discover the diversity of people through our photos that have been taken in natural settings around the world. From South America to Europe.
